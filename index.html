Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?
Just go to the Beginner Lessons section and start with interactive exercises, quizzes, and step-by-step courses.
Is this website free to use?
Yes, most of the lessons and practice materials are free. Some advanced features may require a premium plan.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is needed to browse free lessons, but creating an account helps you save progress, track results, and access personalized learning.
How can I build my English vocabulary?
Practice daily with our word lists, flashcards, and quizzes. You can also explore vocabulary lessons by levels to grow step by step.
Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, after completing structured courses, you will receive a digital certificate to showcase your achievement.
